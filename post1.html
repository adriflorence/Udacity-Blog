Author: adriflorence Title: scientist Date posted: 17 May 2020
Windmill Beach in Simonstown, South Africa, is the location for an amazing, alternative dive. Forget coral reefs and imagine a flooded kelp forest. Shore dive into towering plants and swim with excitable cape fur seals. The stars of the show come out to play, twirl in and out of the kelp and vie for your attention. Their inquisitive behaviour makes this dive particularly special. They aren’t shy of human visitors and will happily swim parallel to your dive course as you make your way around the rocky point. The mischievous mammals only fear one ocean inhabitant, their main predator ­– the great white shark.
